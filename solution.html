Solutions:

Pre-Olympics:

  1. Conduct through cost-benefit analysis
  2. Engage local communities in planning
  3. Develop sustainable infrastructure
  4. Create legacy plans for venues
  5. Establish transport budgeting

During Olympics:

  1. Implement efficient transportation systems
  2. Promote cultural exchange programs
  3. Foster community involvement
  4. Monitor and mitigate environmental impact
  5. Ensure safety and security

Post-Olympics:

  1. Convert venues into public spaces/li>
  2. Repurpose infrastructure for local needs
  3. Maintain upgraded infrastructure
  4. Encourage long-term tourism
  5. Evaluate and share best practices

Innovative solutions:

  1. Modular,temporary venues
  2. Sustainable building materials
  3. Green infrastructure(e.g.,parks,green roofs)
  4. Community-led legacy projects
  5. Digital engagement platforms

Best practices:

  1. Barcelona(1992):Integrated Olympic infrastructure into urban planning
  2. Vancouver(2010):Focused on sustainability and legacy
  3. London(2012):Prioritised community engagement and regeneration
  4. Rio(2016):Implemented modular,temporary venues
  5. Tokyo(2020):Emphasised innovation,sustainability,and community involvement

Conclusion:

By adopting these solutions,host cities can minimise the negative impacts and maximise the benefits of
hosting the Olympics,creating a lasting,positive legacy.
